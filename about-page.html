My Approach To Art

Welcome to my website. I have created physical pieces of art for a number of years. My art reflects my exploration of science and nature, and my view from our place in space and time. We live on an exceptional planet. From earth we look out through time and view an ever-expanding universe, the outward view. We can also see into the subatomic universe, the inward view. We have reached a point in our evolution where we understand the connection between these seemingly different worlds. Where they intersect is what I consider the crossroad.

As humans, we continue to develop science and spirituality based explanations for both of these views. The crossroad is a place where our ability to perceive and understand is quite remarkable. What we think we know blurs as we look farther from what we experience through our senses. These paintings explore the use of color and form at this crossroad of the exceptionally large and small. Thank you for visiting and I hope you enjoyed viewing my art.
